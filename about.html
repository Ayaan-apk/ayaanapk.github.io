Our Mission

At Surrey Hindu Chaplaincy, our mission is to create a nurturing, inclusive environment where students can explore and deepen their spiritual journey. We aim to provide a space that fosters a sense of belonging and community, grounded in the principles of Hindu philosophy and practice. Our chaplaincy is committed to guiding individuals through their spiritual, emotional, and personal growth by offering diverse programs, events, and services tailored to the needs of the Hindu community on campus.

We celebrate and honor the rich tapestry of Hindu culture through year-round events, including major festivals like Diwali, Holi, Navaratri, and Makar Sankranti. These celebrations are more than just events; they are vibrant expressions of our cultural heritage, providing an opportunity for students to connect with age-old traditions, engage in spiritual practices, and experience the joy and togetherness that these festivals bring. Through these events, we aim to educate and inspire a deeper understanding of Hinduism, its values, and its relevance in todayâ€™s world.

Our chaplaincy also facilitates regular spiritual practices such as meditation, yoga, and prayer sessions, fostering mindfulness and inner peace amidst the rigors of academic life. We organize weekly discussions and study groups on sacred texts like the Bhagavad Gita and Upanishads, encouraging thoughtful dialogue and reflection. By providing a supportive network, we empower our members to navigate life's challenges with resilience, grounded in the wisdom and compassion of Hindu teachings.

Whether you are a practicing Hindu, someone curious about the faith, or simply seeking a space for reflection and community, Surrey Hindu Chaplaincy welcomes you. Together, we strive to cultivate an environment of mutual respect, learning, and spiritual growth, where every individual can find peace, purpose, and a sense of community.
